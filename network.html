Network

DOA 's agricultural network relies on close relationships with farmers and consumers to find sustainable and traceable commodities and deliver them around the world. We use our extensive facilities to store, transport and process high value-added commodities. We produce and deliver products in strict compliance with consumer requirements for quality and specifications. A broad understanding of our work gives us the trust of our clients, we biuld strong relationships with all our partners and chase to the quality of work and give confidence in the reliability to our clients. As a responsible and long-term oriented company, we continue to invest and develop our capacities to meet the demands of the modern world.

Partnership

By uniting talents, strong minds and diverse viewpoints, we continue to strengthen partnerships to reach new heights and success. We believe that only by joining forces can we achieve great things. Our company policy is to work openly with all our partners. Direct and transparent relationships allow us all to make long-term plans for the future and to calculate our capabilities.

Logistics

Our extensive logistics network allows us to control our products from grower to consumer. This gives producers the assurance that the quality of their products is maintained and means consumers can have confidence in the origin, quality and delivery of the goods they buy. DOA inspects the goods from a point in the region of origin. We deliver them by road, rail or vessel to and from warehouses, processing plants and port terminals. We have more than 200 rental wagons that allow us to flexibly move large volumes of goods throughout the year. With our global ocean logistics, we manage origin and volume and offer flexible delivery options, on-time delivery and competitive pricing to meet our customers' needs efficiently and quickly.
